Wet beard shave with a straight razor - £15.99

We take extra-great caution with this particular service. For starters, we will ask you if you have any skin allergies or conditions that we should know about. Some people have allergies towards animal brushes, so we would then use a synthethic brush (as opposed to animal brushes) for the purpose of creating a lather. Additional caution will be brought towards looking at the type of products we use for the shave - you will be asked if you prefer a shaving soap or a shaving cream and we can also advise you in case you are unsure. We also have completely vegan options for shaving - by using a synthethic brush and vegan shaving soap/cream.
Furthermore, our barbershop is compliant with British laws and regulations and we always use a new disposable straight-razor blade for every shave, while maintaing all mandatory hygiene, health and safety procedures.
